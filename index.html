TWO LINES. TWO STORIES.

From 2022 to 2024, the cost per wiretap order rose 29.5%—from $180,847 to $234,272. Over that same period, the incrimination rate fell from 12.9% to 11.3%. The two trend lines cross and diverge. More money. Less evidence. The system is degrading.
